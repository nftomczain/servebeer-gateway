DMCA Takedown Policy

ServeBeer IPFS Gateway complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

File a DMCA Takedown Notice

To report copyright infringement, send a notice containing:

  1. Identification of copyrighted work - describe the work being infringed
  2. Infringing material - provide IPFS CID: Qm...
  3. Your contact info - name, address, phone, email
  4. Good faith statement - "I have a good faith belief that use is not authorized"
  5. Accuracy statement - "I swear, under penalty of perjury, this information is accurate"
  6. Your signature - physical or electronic

DMCA Agent Contact:

Form: Submit DMCA Report

Response Time: Within 48 hours

Counter-Notification

If your content was removed and you believe it was a mistake, file a counter-notification with:

  • Your contact information
  • Removed content CID
  • Statement under penalty of perjury that removal was in error
  • Consent to federal court jurisdiction
  • Your signature
Warning: False DMCA claims may result in legal liability under 17 U.S.C. ยง 512(f).

What Happens Next?

  1. We review your notice within 48 hours
  2. If valid, content is unpinned from our nodes
  3. User is notified of the takedown
  4. Content may still exist on other IPFS nodes globally
